# North Star Vision Workshop - Facilitation Plan

## Workshop Overview

**Duration:** 3.5 hours (half-day)
**Format:** Hybrid (Mural + Microsoft Teams)
**Participants:** 5-10 people (Product & Engineering Leadership, team members, strategy support)
**Facilitator Tools:** Mural board, Teams breakout rooms, timer

---

## Workshop Goals

1. Create aligned **North Star Vision** (draft)
2. Define **5-Year Roadmap Themes** (draft)
3. Develop **Capabilities Roadmap** (draft)
4. Identify **Q2 OKR Planning Priorities** (actionable)

---

## Core Challenges to Address

- ✅ Balance ambition with reality
- ✅ Navigate priority conflicts and achieve alignment
- ✅ Translate vision to concrete next steps for Q2 OKR planning

---

## Pre-Work (Due 2-3 days before workshop)

### For All Participants (30 minutes)
1. Complete **Current State Assessment Template** (see separate file)
2. Review existing strategy materials (if available)
3. Bring 2-3 examples of "North Star" products/companies you admire

### For Leadership Team (Additional 15 minutes)
4. Identify top 3 strategic tensions or trade-offs facing the team
5. List non-negotiable constraints (budget, timeline, capabilities)

---

## Workshop Agenda (3.5 hours)

### 🌅 **PHASE 1: Connect & Ground** (30 min)

**09:00-09:15 | Opening & Connection (15 min)**
- Welcome & workshop purpose
- Luma "Check-in": Share one word about your hopes for today
- Review agenda, outputs, and ways of working
- Establish "Ambition vs. Reality" parking lot

**09:15-09:30 | Current State Synthesis (15 min)**
- Facilitator presents synthesis of pre-work
- Highlight key themes from gap analysis and work-in-flight
- Identify alignment areas and tension points
- Q&A and clarifications

---

### 🎯 **PHASE 2: Envision the North Star** (60 min)

**09:30-10:00 | Future-Back Visioning (30 min)**

**Activity: "5 Years from Now" Story**
- *Individual (5 min):* Silent reflection - "It's 2030, we've succeeded beyond our wildest dreams. What does that look like? What impact have we made?"
- *Pairs (10 min):* Share stories in breakout rooms, identify common themes
- *Group (15 min):* Each pair shares top 2 themes, facilitator clusters on Mural

**10:00-10:30 | North Star Vision Drafting (30 min)**

**Activity: Vision Elements Assembly**
- *Small groups (15 min):* 2 groups of 3-5 people draft vision statements using template:
  - **Who** we serve
  - **What** value we create
  - **Why** it matters (impact)
  - **How** we're different
- *Whole group (15 min):*
  - Present both drafts
  - Dot vote on strongest elements
  - Live edit to create unified draft

**Output:** Draft North Star Vision statement

---

### ☕ **BREAK** (10 min)

---

### 🗺️ **PHASE 3: Map the Journey** (70 min)

**10:40-11:20 | 5-Year Roadmap Themes (40 min)**

**Activity: Horizons Mapping**
- *Facilitator introduces (5 min):* 3 horizons framework
  - Year 1-2: Foundation
  - Year 3-4: Scale
  - Year 5: Transformation

- *Individual brainstorm (5 min):* What strategic themes/bets belong in each horizon?

- *Group clustering (15 min):*
  - Add stickies to Mural board
  - Facilitator groups similar themes
  - Dot vote to prioritize top 3-4 themes per horizon

- *Sequencing (15 min):*
  - Discuss dependencies between themes
  - Identify "must-haves" vs "nice-to-haves"
  - Reality check against constraints from pre-work

**11:20-11:50 | Capabilities Roadmap (30 min)**

**Activity: Capability Mapping**
- *Small groups (15 min):* Each group takes 1-2 roadmap themes
  - What capabilities (tech, people, process, partnerships) are needed?
  - When do we need them? (map to timeline)
  - What's the gap from today?

- *Gallery walk (10 min):* Groups review each other's work, add missing items

- *Prioritization (5 min):* Dot vote on critical capabilities for Year 1-2

**Output:** Draft 5-Year Roadmap Themes + Draft Capabilities Roadmap

---

### 🚀 **PHASE 4: Translate to Action** (50 min)

**11:50-12:20 | Q2 OKR Priority Setting (30 min)**

**Activity: Vision-to-Action Cascade**
- *Facilitator introduces (5 min):* "Working backwards" - What must be true in Q2 to achieve Year 1 goals?

- *Individual reflection (5 min):* Based on everything discussed, what are the 1-2 most critical things to prioritize in Q2?

- *Group discussion (20 min):*
  - Share priorities, cluster themes
  - Apply prioritization criteria:
    1. Directly enables North Star vision
    2. Addresses critical capability gap
    3. Feasible in Q2 timeframe
    4. High impact / effort ratio
  - Narrow to top 3-5 Q2 focus areas
  - Identify owners for OKR development

**12:20-12:30 | Closing & Next Steps (10 min)**

**Activity: Commitment & Clarity**
- Review all outputs created today
- Assign owners for refining drafts (who, when)
- Schedule follow-up for OKR planning
- Luma "Check-out": One word about how you're feeling now
- Appreciations and close

**Output:** Q2 Priority Focus Areas + Owner Assignments

## Facilitation Principles (Luma-Inspired)

### 1. **Connection Before Content**
- Start with check-in to build psychological safety
- Use breakout pairs for generative activities
- End with check-out for reflection

### 2. **Energy Management**
- Vary activity types: solo → pairs → small group → whole group
- Include movement (gallery walk, stand/stretch break)
- Strategic 10-min break at midpoint

### 3. **Productive Conflict**
- Name tensions explicitly (ambition vs. reality)
- Use parking lot for scope creep
- Dot voting to democratize decision-making

### 4. **Visual Thinking**
- All activities happen on Mural board
- Use templates, colors, icons for clarity
- Make thinking visible in real-time

### 5. **Bias Toward Action**
- Every phase produces concrete output
- Assign owners and deadlines before closing
- Connect vision directly to Q2 OKRs

## Success Metrics

**During Workshop:**
- All participants contribute in at least 3 activities
- Consensus reached (not just compromise) on North Star vision
- Energy levels stay high (check-in at break and closing)

**After Workshop:**
- Draft vision, roadmap, and capabilities documented within 48 hours
- Q2 OKR planning kickoff scheduled within 1 week
- 80%+ participant satisfaction (post-workshop survey)

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|---------------------|
| **Scope creep** (trying to cover too much) | Use parking lot aggressively; timebox all activities; facilitator keeps focus on outputs |
| **Priority conflicts escalate** | Pre-surface tensions in opening; use structured decision frameworks; escalate unresolved items to follow-up |
| **Tech issues (hybrid)** | Test all tools beforehand; have backup plan (phone dial-in, Google Docs); assign tech support person |
| **Dominant voices** | Use silent individual work first; breakout pairs; dot voting; explicitly invite quiet voices |
| **Can't reach consensus** | Use "disagree and commit" framework; identify what decisions can be made vs. need more input; parking lot |
| **Running over time** | Build 5-min buffer into each phase; be willing to cut gallery walk or reduce discussion time; prioritize outputs in order |

## Adaptation Notes

**If you have more time (full day):**
- Add deeper gap analysis workshop component
- Include customer/user research review
- Build detailed Year 1 quarterly roadmap
- Do scenario planning exercises

**If you have less time (2 hours):**
- Focus on vision + Year 1 themes only
- Skip capabilities roadmap (do as pre-work for next session)
- Reduce individual reflection time
- Combine phases 3 and 4

**If group is larger (15+ people):**
- Add co-facilitator
- Use more breakout groups
- Extend time for report-outs
- Consider pre-work to align on vision elements
