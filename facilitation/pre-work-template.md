## Tips for Completing Pre-Work

- **Be honest:** Candid assessment is more valuable than diplomatic answers
- **Be specific:** Examples and details help more than generalities
- **Think big:** This is your chance to influence long-term strategy
- **Don't overthink:** First instincts are often the most valuable
- **Budget time:** Set aside 30-45 uninterrupted minutes to reflect deeply
