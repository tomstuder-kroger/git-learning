**Elements to include:**

## Facilitation Tips for Mural

1. **Use the Outline View** to quickly navigate between frames
2. **Summon participants** to keep everyone on the same frame
3. **Set timers visibly** so participants can pace themselves
4. **Use facilitator cursor** (spotlight) to draw attention
